Welcome!

My work-related experience is centered around technical consulting, academic advisement, youth development, retail, management, and customer service. Being a Mobile Expert/Apple Master introduced technical support to my repertoire only to be enhanced after becoming a Geek Squad Agent shortly after increasing my skill set with troubleshooting, software repairs, device set ups, and data transfers. As an University Peer Academic Coach at Fayetteville State University, I specialized in time management, goal orientation, individualized lesson planning, and tips in various academic areas (math, writing/essays, etc). I gained experience in youth development while serving as Vice President of a nonprofit mentoring, R.O.O.T.S (Regiment Of Overriding The Statistics). R.O.O.T.S primary focus was exposing at-risk youth to beneficial alternative routes after graduation from high school. I've received retail, customer service, and management experience from Journey Kidz serving as a part-time sales associate then promoted to Sales Lead/Key Holder. My academic achievements consist of graduating from Cross Creek Early College High School with 66 college credits, becoming a McNair Scholar researcher where I focused on Stereotype Threat and African American English in Academic Setting and how the two factors influence academic performance. I received my Bachelor's Degree in Psychology From Fayetteville State University. I transitioned from the professor track to become a full-stack software developer which started at App Academy.
